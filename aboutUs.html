About Us

"Muslim Guider is a handy app for Muslims to stay connected to their faith throughout the day. Find prayer times accurately wherever you are in Malaysia using the Prayer Time. The app also features a built-in Qibla Finder, guiding you towards the direction of the Kaaba for your prayers. Locate mosques near your current location with ease using the Mosque Locator, ensuring you have a place for congregation when needed. Finally, explore a collection of Hadith, the sayings and teachings of Prophet Muhammad, to strengthen your understanding of Islam."

Qibla Finder

"Qibla Finder Ease The user of the app to find the correct position of qibla to ensure the qibla is in the right direction. You can choose to either get the location using the gps of your device by clicking Get Qibla Direction or you can search your preffered location using Search Bar."

Mosque Locator

"Mosque Locator will help you find the nearest mosque to your current location by using gps of your device ,the app will request for permission to use your location or you can search your preffered location using Search Bar .

Prayer Time

"Prayer Time will make sure you always get the precise prayer time for subuh,zuhr,asr,maghrib and isha at all time in Malaysia. The app will request for permission to use your location or you can search your preffered location using Search Bar "

Hadith

"Hadith contains a collection of Hadith, the sayings and teachings of Prophet Muhammad, to strengthen your understanding of Islam. you can search for various hadith from many hadith writer such as Musnad Ahmad , Imam Malik, Sahih Muslim and many more "
